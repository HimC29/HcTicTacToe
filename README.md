# hc!TicTacToe

[![hc!TicTacToe](https://img.shields.io/badge/hc!TicTacToe-TicTacToe%20Game-blue)](https://himc29.github.io/hcTicTacToe/)

hc!TicTacToe is a simple and interactive web-based Tic-Tac-Toe game with a Pass and Play feature to play with your friends and also a Play with Robot feature that lets you play with bots ranging from a easy level to a hard, unbeatable level with Minimax AI, all built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and directly playable in the browser.

Check it out live: [hc!TicTacToe Website](https://himc29.github.io/hcTicTacToe/)  

---

## Features

- **Pass and play** – Play with your friend by taking turns to play.  
- **Play with robot** – Play with a robot that has a range of many skill levels.  
- **Game end checking** – Checks when the game ends, wheter X or O wins, or a tie.  

---

## How to Play

Visit the website:  

[https://himc29.github.io/hcTicTacToe/](https://himc29.github.io/hcTicTacToe/)  

- Select between the Pass and Play and Play with Robot options and click START GAME.  
- Press on the cells to place your Xs or Os there.  
- Try to get three in a row to win.  

You don’t need to install anything—everything runs directly in your browser.
